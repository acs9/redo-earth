Clean and sustainable energy solutions in South East Asia

REDO EARTH

Renewable Energy Development and Operation (REDO) Earth is an Independent Power Producer (IPP) that aims to generate clean, natural and sustainable energy solutions; enhancing the electrical generation capacity with zero carbon emissions and thereby contributing to the mitigation of global climate change.

REDO's objective is to utilize potential renewable energy in South East Asia to the maximum extent and reduce or eliminate the dependency over polluting power generation sources.

REDO is a new start-up by a team of highly talented individuals with proven capabilities and varied domain expertise, with over 20-years of combined work experience across industries including Renewable Energy industry.

We are highly interested in Developing, Investing, Constructing, Owning and Operating Renewable energy projects in South East Asia.
